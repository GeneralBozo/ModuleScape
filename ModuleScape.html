This map has been designed in order to help depict the display village that will be created to show off the nature of the BuildStacks. While all buildings may be connected via a footpath to the main road, it is still only the depiction of a display village and because of that there is a parking space provided for people who might want to look through the designs interiors andwalk around the environment, while also provided roads for a quick drive, meaning visitors who may be too busy to stop and give a lot of time are still able to drive past and view facades and dimensions of the buildings.
This is only an exemplar image of a BuildStack, created solely for presentation and representation purposes. It is meant to depict both the modular and stackable nature of these buildings, highlighting the numerous ways that they can be utilised to create a unique, individual space for a person to live in. It draws attention to the simple nature of the building which can be further added to by customers at their own discretion if they would prefer a more complex look. All of these materials were chosen by the designer simply to demonstrate one possible option, which we would like to emphasise, and are not necessarily what the building has to look like.
